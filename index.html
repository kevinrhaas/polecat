Customizable arbitration

Consensus isn't a black box. Choose how the answer is formed, name which model gets the final say, or turn it off to just compare side by side.

  • Best Answer — pick the single strongest, lightly corrected.
  • Comprehensive — merge everything into the most complete answer.
  • Validated Synthesis — merge, but cross-check claims and flag disagreements.
  • Debate & Synthesize — models critique each other, then synthesize.
  • Sequential Refinement — each model refines a running draft.

Edit the prompts, save your own strategies, and export/import your whole setup.
